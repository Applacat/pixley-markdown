# Ideas: AI + Pixley Workflows

Ask your AI to generate these as interactive markdown. Open in Pixley. Respond through the document.

## Code & Engineering

- **QA checklists** for code review — AI generates test cases as checkboxes, you check off what passes
- **Architecture decision records** — AI lays out options as radio choices, you pick and leave comments
- **Release readiness gates** — review blocks with PASS/FAIL for each criteria
- **Bug triage templates** — fill-in fields for severity, repro steps, and owner assignment
- **Migration checklists** — step-by-step checkboxes for database migrations or API upgrades
- **Sprint retrospectives** — AI drafts discussion points, team leaves inline feedback

## Product & Design

- **Feature specs** — AI writes the spec, you approve or reject sections with suggested edits
- **Design review documents** — AI proposes copy changes, you accept/reject each suggestion
- **User story refinement** — fill-in fields for acceptance criteria, effort choices for story points
- **Competitive analysis** — AI researches and writes findings, you comment on what matters
- **Prioritization matrices** — radio choices for impact vs effort on each feature

## Writing & Research

- **Outline review** — AI drafts an outline, you leave comments and approve sections
- **Fact-checking worksheets** — AI makes claims, you verify with PASS/FAIL review gates
- **Interview prep** — fill-in fields for your answers to common questions
- **Content calendars** — status pipelines (idea / draft / review / published) per piece
- **Literature reviews** — AI summarizes papers, you leave inline comments on relevance

## How It Works

1. Prompt your AI: *"Write a QA checklist for [feature] using Pixley interactive markdown"*
2. Paste the `06-For-AI-Agents` prompt template into your AI's context
3. Save the output as a `.md` file
4. Open in Pixley — click, fill in, approve, comment
5. AI reads your responses from the file and iterates
