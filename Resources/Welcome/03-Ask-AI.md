# AI Chat

When you're reading a document, you can ask questions about it using the AI chat panel.

## How to Use It

1. **Open the chat panel** — Click the chat bubble icon in the toolbar (top right)
2. **Type your question** — Ask anything about the document you're viewing
3. **Press Enter** — The AI will answer using the document as context

## What Can You Ask?

- "Summarize this document"
- "What are the main points?"
- "Explain the technical terms"
- "What does [specific section] mean?"
- "Are there any action items?"

## Context-Aware Answers

The AI reads the document you're viewing and uses it to answer your questions. The answers are specific to your content, not generic responses.

---

*Tip: The app uses Apple Intelligence, which runs entirely on your device — your files stay private.*
