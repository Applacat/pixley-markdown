# AI Chat

Open the chat panel with `⌘⇧A`. Ask questions about whatever document you're reading.

## Good Questions

- "What is this document about?"
- "Summarize the key decisions made so far"
- "Which QA gates are still pending?"
- "What action items are left?"

The AI can also interact with the document — try "check off the first three items" or "mark the build verification as passed."

## How It Works

Pixley uses **Apple Intelligence**, which runs entirely on your Mac. Nothing is sent to a server. No cloud, no API keys, no data leaves your device.

> To enable: **System Settings > Apple Intelligence & Siri**

## Tips

- Specific questions work better than "summarize everything"
- The conversation resets periodically to stay accurate — an orange banner lets you know
- Press **Escape** or click **Forget** to clear manually
- Switch documents and the AI picks up context from the new file
