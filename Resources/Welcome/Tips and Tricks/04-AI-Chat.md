# AI Chat

Open the chat panel with the toolbar icon or `⌘⇧A`. Type a question about the document you're reading and press Enter.

## What to Ask

The AI reads your current document and answers based on what's in it. Some good starting points:

- "What is this document about?"
- "Summarize the key points"
- "What does this function do?"
- "Are there any action items?"
- "Explain this in simpler terms"

Follow-ups work naturally — "Tell me more about that" or "Can you put that in a table?"

## How It Works

The AI uses **Apple Intelligence**, which runs entirely on your Mac. Nothing is sent to a server. No cloud, no API keys, no data leaves your device.

> To enable Apple Intelligence, go to **System Settings → Apple Intelligence & Siri**.

## Context Limits

The on-device model has a limited context window. A few things to know:

- After **3 exchanges**, the conversation resets automatically to keep answers accurate
- An orange banner appears when this happens
- For long documents, the AI focuses on the first ~2,500 characters
- **Specific questions** work better than "summarize everything"
- Press **Escape** or click **Forget** to clear the conversation manually
