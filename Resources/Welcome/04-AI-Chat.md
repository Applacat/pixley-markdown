# AI Chat

When viewing a document, you can open the AI Chat panel to ask questions about it.

## Opening the Chat

Click the **speech bubbles** icon in the top-right toolbar. The chat panel slides in from the right.

## Asking Questions

Type your question and press Enter. The AI will read your document and answer based on what's in it.

Good questions to ask:
- "What is this document about?"
- "Summarize the key points"
- "What does [term] mean in this context?"
- "Are there any action items?"

## Follow-Up Questions

The AI remembers your conversation. You can ask follow-up questions like:
- "Can you put that in a table?"
- "Tell me more about the second point"
- "What else should I know?"

## The Memory Meter

At the top of the chat, you'll see a **Memory** gauge. This shows how much of the AI's "brain" is being used:

- **Green** — Plenty of room
- **Orange** — Getting full
- **Red** — Near the limit

When the meter fills up, the AI might struggle to remember earlier parts of the conversation.

## Forgetting

Click **Forget** (or press **ESC**) to clear the conversation and reset the memory meter. This starts fresh with the full document context.

---

*Tip: For very long documents, the AI only sees the first ~8,000 characters. Follow-up questions use less context so the chat can be more conversational.*
