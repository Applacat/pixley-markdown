# About Pixley

Pixley is made by **Applacat LLC**, a small studio building tools that make your Mac work better.

## The Idea

AI generates markdown. You read it. Pixley lets you respond *through* the document — checkboxes, choices, fill-ins, comments, approvals — all saving back to the `.md` file. No editing, just clicking.

No editor. No note-taking app. Just a relay between you and AI, with markdown as the shared medium.
